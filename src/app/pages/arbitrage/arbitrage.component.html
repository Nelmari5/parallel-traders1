Arbitrage Service

The Parallel Arbitrage Service in partnership with Ovex is a product that is designed to exploit mismatches in the price of Bitcoin in South African Rands (ZAR). Over the past 4 years, BTC/ZAR markets have traded at an average premium of 4.6% to BTC/USD markets.

The Arbitrage Service enables Parallel customers from South Africa to use their annual Single Discretionary Allowance (SDA) of R1 million and Foreign Investment Allowance (FIA) up to an additional R10 million to earn arbitrage trading profits.

Complete the three steps below to get access to the Arbitrage Service.

Please note that you will need a valid South African ID Book/Card in order to complete the application process, and a minimum capital amount of R50,000.

  • Submit Account Information
  • Upload Proof of Identity Documents
  • Upload Proof of Address Document
  • Upload Source of Funds Documents
